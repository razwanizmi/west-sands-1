THE ISLAND

WELCOME TO UKULHAS

Located in North Ari Atoll, Ukulhas is a tiny 57 acre island. It offers unparalled beauty - with one of the most beautiful beaches in the area, combined with thick natural vegetations. Surrounding it are few of the most spectacular diving and marine life sites in Maldives.
